EOB EOB

What is the EOB?
The EOB is an innovative tool developed by Pro Control to provide remote support. It stands for "Engineer out of the Box" and allows engineers to remotely troubleshoot issues by guiding the on-site employee through audio, video, and chat. This is a cost-effective, environmentally-friendly, and secure solution that is becoming increasingly popular among businesses for providing quick and efficient support to customers.

How does the EOB work?
The EOB works through a combination of hardware and software. On-site, a camera, microphone, and audio/video communication device are installed so that the remote engineer can see and hear what is happening. A secure network connection is established for data exchange. Additionally, there is also a chat function available to send data. The security of data is maximally ensured, making the EOB a handy and secure tool for businesses that depend on fast and efficient problem-solving.

What are the benefits of using the EOB?
The EOB has many benefits, including:
1. Remote support: Provides a remote support option that eliminates the need for on-site visits, saving time and money.
2. Cost-effective: Is a cost-effective solution for businesses looking to provide efficient support to customers.
3. Environmentally-friendly: Reduces carbon footprint by minimizing travel and transportation.
4. Secure: Provides maximum data security during remote communication.
5. Overall, the EOB is a powerful tool for businesses that rely on efficient problem-solving and customer support.

Why Choose Us

Sustainable and Cost-Effective

On-site visits can be time-consuming, expensive, and harmful to the environment. The EOB enables remote support, reducing the need for physical visits and thus cutting down on costs and emissions.

User-Friendly and Flexible

With its intuitive design and easy installation, the EOB is user-friendly and flexible, allowing you to provide support to employees around the world quickly and efficiently.

Secure and Private

The EOB uses a secure system to ensure maximum privacy and data protection. You can be confident that all communication and data exchange will be safeguarded, even when dealing with sensitive information or supporting employees in remote locations.
