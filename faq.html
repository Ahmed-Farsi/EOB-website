Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account?

To create an account, go to the registration page and fill out the form with your details.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription by logging into your account and going to the 'Subscriptions' page. Then, click on 'Cancel' next to the subscription you wish to terminate.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via the contact form on our website or by sending an email to support@eob.com.
